Your Time

After collecting the documents you'll need to register, the process should take about 30 minutes.

What You'll Need

You will need these documents or information about your business to get registered.

  • Company legal name and DBA (doing business as) name
  • Primary industry in which you do business such as "Home Electronics" or "General Plumbing"
  • Main physical address
  • Contact information such as phone numbers and email address
  • Details for the person will be the Administrator for your ServiceLive account. We assume that's you!
  • Information on your business insurance. Scan and upload your insurance certificate and we can automatically populate some of your data!
  • Information for your first Pro. If you're not going out and fulfilling on service, you'll need to enter at least one Pro who will get a background check.
