Background Screen

As an Admin you have indicated you wil perform work in the ServiceLive Platform. To get work routed to your company you need to complete a background check.

Why do we do background checks?

Since most service orders require a home visit, to ensure continued highest quality we require that all of our Service Pro undergo a background check. This requires these easy steps:

  1. Explain the process and gain approval from the Service Pro to submit their background check.
  2. Click the Perform Background Check button to open the background check process form. Complete and submit.
  3. Your team member's status will be automatically update when the background check results come back. Average turn around time for results is 7-10 days.

NOTE: Administrators not performing work in the field and Dispatchers are not required to do a background check.
